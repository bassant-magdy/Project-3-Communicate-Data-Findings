Ford Gobike Data Exploration¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the characteristics of Bike share and thier features, The main focus was on the duration of trip, age, user type and gender.

Dataset Overview¶

This document explores a dataset containing the trip data of the ford and it is the first regional and large-scale bicycle sharing system deployed in California and on the West Coast of the United States.

Distribution of Trip Durations¶

Trip duration is in second and in the dataset taking on a very large range of values so We can not see clearly which the data looks highly skewed with a very long tail of data points, so we will Scale the x-axis to log-type scales for more clear vision. we will observe that Number of Trips increases starting from around 8000 to 12000 values, then starts to fall down around to 2000 values approximately. The duration of the trip is approximately centered on values which are less than 2000 seconds with peak around 500 seconds.

Distribution of day of week by user type and Distribution of hour of days by user type¶

There are much more subscriber users than customers users. The large drop of bars on weekends days (Sat and Sun) for subscribers indicates that they mainly used bikes for work commute during work days. Subscriber user increases on rush hours when people go to work at 8 and 9 AM and getting off work at 17 and 18 PM, double confirmed their usage purpose and goal of riding. Similar pattern was not observed among customers who tend to ride most in the afternoon or early evening as for a different purpose than the subscriber riders.

Hourly Usage during Weekdays by Customers and Subscribers¶

The next plot shows obviously very different usage between the two type of users. Subscribers use the system profusely on work days (Monday to Friday) while customers use it a lot on weekends, especially in the afternoon. Many trips concentrated around 8 and 8 AM and 17 and 18 PM on work days for subscribers when used for work transport, yet customers tend to use more in the late afternoon approx. 17 PM Monday to Friday.
